What is CNN?

Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) are a class of deep learning models, primarily used for image processing, classification, and segmentation tasks. They are designed to automatically and adaptively learn spatial hierarchies of features from the input data.

Handwritten Digit Recognition

Handwritten digit recognition is a task that involves identifying digits from handwritten images. This is a common problem in the field of computer vision and has applications in various domains, including banking, postal services, and more.

Accuracy Measures

The accuracy of handwritten digit recognition models is typically measured using metrics such as accuracy, precision, recall, and the F1 score. These metrics help in understanding the performance of the model in terms of correctly identifying digits and minimizing errors.
